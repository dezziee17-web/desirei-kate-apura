Promotional Campaign

TastyBitz

Year: 2026
Category: Poster
Description:
A promotional campaign for TastyBitz, a local drink spot in Tanauan, Leyte with an Instagram post layout that features a bright, eye-catching photo of me holding their signature mango graham shake.
Its purpose is to boost the business by attracting more customers through a limited-time 10% discount promo, encouraging people to enjoy affordable, tasty treats while supporting a community-based shop that started small (from a home garage in 2020) and grew into a physical store.
